Welcome!

Rachel Poppen is a Literary Studies M.A. student at the University of Iowa. She previously graduated from The University of Texas at Austin with a Master of Science in Information Studies (MSIS) and has worked professionally as an archivist. Her research interests include Mediveal and Early Modern book history, the patron/archives relationship, and primary source instruction.
